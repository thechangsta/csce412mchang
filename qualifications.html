Midcontinent Independent Systems Operator (MISO)

Summer 2022 - Eagan, MN
This past summer, I had the opportunity to be an intern in the Operations Modeling team at MISO. I worked on a number of projects related to improving the model of the electric grid for the Midwest US including data validations, a CSV enhancement for Siemens, improvements to a Python application called CLAW, and determining mappings between two databases. I started off testing PL/SQL data validations which ensure the quality of the modeling database. I ended up mentoring another intern in the testing process too. My main contribution to MISO was an improvement to an internal Python Application which suggested mappings between two databases. I was able narrow down and improve the quality of suggestions, saving the end user many hours of verifying mappings.
